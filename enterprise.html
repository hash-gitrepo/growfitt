Screen 04 / Execution Levers

What should you actually do?

Ranked execution levers prioritised by GFS impact, time to effect, and confidence. Not all actions are equal — sequence matters.

Fix ICP definition first — you recover 60% of the efficiency loss from a single lever.
#1 ⭐ Top pick
Tighten ICP definition
Redefine your Ideal Customer Profile to exclude deal profiles with cycles >45 days. Introduce ICP scoring at top-of-funnel to disqualify low-fit opportunities early. This directly improves win rate and compresses sales cycle — the two primary drivers of CAC inflation.
